Some things you can do within the text of a chapter.


There are many things you may want to do within your text. ePubChef will do several things automatically. For example, it will change boring straight quotes to smart quotes as in the following paragraph. The tool will create a new paragraph after each blank line in your text. A setting in the recipe will create drop-caps for the first letter of each chapter.

  • put text in italics
  • create a list like this one

She said, “this is a quote.” Then she followed with, “another quotation.”

“What is this quotation about?” she said.

If you prefer single quotes: She said, ‘this is a quote.’ Then she followed with, ‘another quotation.’

‘What is this quotation about?’ she said.

  • characters such as ampersand are dealt with correctly: &
  • There was a double space removed before the word double.
  • This line began with a tab which has been removed.
  • There's a single quote here which was converted to a smart quote.
